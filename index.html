The Mob Quiz: Louis Daidone

When you Google my last name, "Daidone," the first image that comes up on Google images is a mug shot of a middle-aged man. His name is Louis Daidone, otherwise known as Louis "Bagel" Daidone, and he's a New York mobster who was formerly the head of the "Lucchese Crime Family." I say formerly because he's know in a high-security penitentiary in Pennsylvania for many reasons, one is conspiracy to murder.
